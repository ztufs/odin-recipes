Spaghetti ala moi

Home

This is a recipe I learned myself, which I am really fond of.
I really enjoy it, and I hope you do to.

Ingredients

  • 400g grounded chicken
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 mushrooms sliced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 cube of chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Chop the mushrooms and start frying them on medium heat.
    Cut the garlic and fry it in a bowl along with the tomato paste for 4 minutes.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, chicken broth and seasonings to the bowl.
  3. When the shrooms are finished, add them to the sauce.
    Boil water, and add the pasta.
  4. Fry grounded chicken, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the meat to the sauce, and boil on medium heat for 10 minutes.
